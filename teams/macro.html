MACRO DIVISION


Last year, our Macro Research Team was expanded from an apply-to-join team to one available to all members who have previously pitched and are interested in macroeconomic investing.

In 2022/23, several groups created reports and presentations on various macroeconomic topics, including conditional reports on political events such as the changes to tensions between China and Taiwan or energy and Scottish Independence, an examination of uranium commodities, and an exploration of trends in the S&P 500. These reports were presented to the wider EUTIC community and fund at the end of each semester.

The expansion of our Macro Research Team has allowed us to deepen our coverage of key macro themes and broaden our reach within EUTIC. We have seen a significant increase in interest and engagement in members who have pitched during their first year at EUTIC but wanted to remain at EUTIC to further their interests and continue contributing in some capacity.

Next year, we aim for the Macro Research Heads to produce material with key macro points that include interest rate predictions, central bank updates, current events relating to macro, and broad updates on certain sectors, with input from members of the team. These slides will be regularly updated to reflect changes in the macroeconomic environment and are used as a tool for guiding pitching in sectors and portfolio management decisions.

What we do

At Women in EUTIC (WEUTIC), we are dedicated to creating an inclusive and supportive environment for our female members. Our mission is to empower women in finance by offering resources, opportunities, and connections that help them excel within EUTIC and beyond.

Our impact

  1. Empowering Female Leadership
  2. Enhanced Career Opportunities
  3. Building a Supportive Community

Through our initiatives, WEUTIC has successfully nurtured and inspired members to assume leadership positions within the club. By promoting women's representation in leadership roles, we're helping to create a more inclusive and diverse environment within the finance community.

Our workshops and networking events have had a tangible impact on our members' career trajectories. Many of our female EUTIC members have secured competitive internships and job placements in the finance industry, thanks to the resources and connections provided.

By fostering collaboration among other women societies and offering networking and social events, WEUTIC has created a strong support network for women pursuing careers in finance. This sense of community and solidarity empowers our members to navigate challenges and advance their careers with confidence.

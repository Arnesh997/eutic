Women in EUTIC

Women in EUTIC aims to create a network for the female members by giving you the opportunity to collaborate with likeminded individuals at the University of Edinburgh

Join us at Women in EUTIC to participate in:

1) Discussions with inspiring female alumnus from the industry.

2) Career development tailored towards women.

3) Networking Events with other women

Take this opportunity to push forward with your goals to build your future in the financial industry.

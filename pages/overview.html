Overview

Welcome to the Edinburgh University
Trading and Investment Club (EUTIC).
Celebrating 22 years of experience, EUTIC
is now the largest and most successful
student-run real money investment portfolio
in the UK and Europe, worth over £58,000.
We also have over 500 members,
making us one of the largest non-sporting societies
in Edinburgh.

Activities

EUTIC holds a weekly club-wide meeting that gives members the opportunity to learn more about finance and current affairs. The line-up includes fund updates, debates, educational talks, as well as external and internal speakers.

EUTIC is lucky to have a fantastic range of industry-leading sponsors, each with a commitment to attracting the best talent to their firms. By attending our sponsor events, you will be able to meet professionals and find out more about possible gradate employment opportunities.

We promote events such as CV improvement workshops, networking events, company presentations and alumni talks for members insterested in pursuing a career in the financial services industry. In fact, more than 65% of our members have secured spring weeks and/or internships in the financial services industry.

EUTIC, with 9 sectors, an annual conference, and various networking events, gives you the perfect chance to meet other students as well as professionals. Our social events throughout the year, such as our Christmas Banquet and Annual Gala are a great place to relax and celebrate the work done throughout the year.

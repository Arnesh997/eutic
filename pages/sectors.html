Sectors

The EUTIC Fund is managed by 9 dedicated sectors, each focusing on a different research area. By joining a sector you can get involved in managing the fund, looking into an industry that you're passionate about.

EUTIC provides you with a unique opportunity to learn about investment at no personal risk as well as meeting new people. Drop by one of the weekly sector meetings and join the conversation!

Our members actively participate in the investment of the EUTIC fund by undertaking roles as analysts in a given research sector.

Alternative

Basic Materials

The Basic Materials sector is at the heart of the global economy, encompassing stocks that engage in the discovery, development and processing of raw materials. Our focus is to analyse key political and macroeconomic drivers and predict how they will impact our industry in order to maximise returns.

Consumer

In Consumer we can invest into anything you can buy. We look at consumption patterns and macroeconomic trends that effect companies specializing in consumer goods. These companies have links to retail, technology, industrial production and other goods an individual can purchase.

Energy & Utilities

The Energy & Utilities Sector looks at the key political, economic and financial drivers of one of the biggest, and most dynamic industries in the world.

Financial

The Financial Sector is a highly cyclical section of the economy made up of firms that provide financial services to commercial and retail customers. This includes investment banks, commercial banks, hedge funds and insurance companies.

Healthcare

The healthcare sector is diverse and evolving, combining innovations at the forefront of TMT, scientific research and consumer industries to improve the lives of everyone, everyday, everywhere.

Industrial

The Industrial Sector provides goods and services used in construction, manufacturing and by the military. From industrial robots operated by AI to spacecraft that will make space travel a reality, the sector stands at the forefront of innovation.

TMT

TMT stands for technology, media and telecommunications and encompasses various big and exciting companies such as Apple and Amazon. Additionally, TMT was the best performing sector of 2017/8.

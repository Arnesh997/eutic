Welcome On Board

Join us via Edinburgh University Students' Association

Next steps after joining:

The first thing you have to do after purchasing a membership is to join the main Facebook group-chat.

In the group-chat, we regularly update any information regarding our weekly general meetings, career events, and socials.

After joining the main Facebook page, if you would like to join a specific sector, you must join the Facebook page of the chosen sector and start attending the meetings that are posted on that page.

If you would like to join the Trading Division, you must join the Facebook Page of the Trading Division and start attending the meetings posted on the page.

For the other teams (Legal Team, Macro Research Team), you must apply whenever the applications for the teams start.

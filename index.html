Message from the President

With over 400 active members and a substantial £79,000 in assets under management, the Edinburgh University Trading and Investment Club (EUTIC) stands as the largest student-run fund in the UK. Our society is built on a foundation of diversity and excellence, welcoming individuals from various backgrounds and fields of study.

EUTIC is committed to equipping our members with knowledge about the financial world, inspiring them to explore careers in the financial services industry. In recent years, our performance and membership have reached new heights, thanks to the dedication of our existing members and a shared commitment to our core mission.

We prioritize giving back to our community through annual charity donations funded by a percentage of our profits. Furthermore, our various sectors operate within an Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) framework, reflecting our dedication to responsible investing. We even have an impact investing sector focused on investments with the potential for positive social change.

With over 50% of our members coming from diverse academic backgrounds, EUTIC serves as a prominent platform for students from across the university. We aim to enhance your experience by offering a wide range of activities and opportunities to develop your financial knowledge and skills.

As we continue to expand our initiatives and improve the experience for our members, we are eager to welcome individuals interested in finance, investment, and trading from all academic disciplines. Whether you're studying in the Business School, the School of Economics, or any other field, EUTIC offers a supportive and inclusive environment for you to learn, network, and grow.

We invite you to explore our society and its activities. Your engagement with EUTIC will not only contribute to your personal and professional growth but will also have a positive impact on our growing community. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you reach new heights in your academic and career pursuits.

Yours Sincerely,
Valeriya Tektel
EUTIC President

General Meetings

EUTIC holds a weekly club-wide meeting that gives members the opportunity to learn more about finance and current affairs. The line-up includes fund updates, debates, educational talks, as well as external and internal speakers.

Sector Meetings

Our fund is managed through 9 dedicated sectors, each focusing on a different industry. Sector meetings allow members to learn more about their industry of choice as well as learn key technical skills, including financial modelling.

Sponsor Events

EUTIC is lucky to have a fantastic range of industry-leading sponsors, each with a commitment to attracting the best talent to their firms. By attending our sponsor events, members meet professionals and find out more about possible internship and graduate opportunities.

Socials

From our annual Edinburgh Finance Conference to the Masquerade Ball, we host over 100 events annually. Our weekly socials also ensure that our members are well connected with one another, fusing learning with a bit of fun.

Pitches

Members have the opportunity to undertake roles as senior and junior analysts in their sectors, giving them the chance to research potential investments, analyse company financials, and interpret current economic conditions to project future performance. Stocks and bonds are then pitched to our investment panel.

Career Options

We promote events such as CV and cover letter improvement workshops, networking events, company presentations, and alumni talks for members interested in pursuing a career in the financial services industry.
